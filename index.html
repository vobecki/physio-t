Our services

What we treat

Comprehensive physiotherapy services for all ages — from weekend warriors to those recovering from surgery.

Sports injury rehabilitation

Sports injuries

🏃

What we treat

  • ACL, MCL & ligament injuries
  • Ankle sprains & strains
  • Runner's knee & shin splints
  • Tennis elbow & rotator cuff
  • Concussion management
📋

How we help

Sport-specific rehab programs, return-to-play assessment, strength & conditioning guidance, and injury prevention strategies to keep you in the game.

Back pain & spine care

Back & neck pain

💆

What we treat

  • Lower back pain & sciatica
  • Neck pain & whiplash
  • Herniated discs
  • Postural pain from sitting
  • Spinal stenosis
🦴

How we help

Manual therapy, spinal mobilization, core strengthening, posture correction, and ergonomic advice for work and home.

Post-surgery recovery

Post-operative rehab

🏥

What we treat

  • Knee & hip replacements
  • ACL reconstruction
  • Fracture recovery
  • Spinal surgery rehab
  • Rotator cuff repair
⏳

How we help

Gradual, guided recovery plans that respect surgical protocols while maximizing your return to function. We work closely with your surgeon.

Workplace injury care

Work injuries

🏢

What we treat

  • Repetitive strain injuries
  • Back injuries from lifting
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Falls & impact injuries
  • Overuse injuries
🛡️

How we help

Workplace ergonomic assessments, injury prevention training, return-to-work planning, and documentation for insurance claims.
